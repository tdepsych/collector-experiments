Pac-Man


Use the arrow keys to move Pac-Man around the maze to eat pellets and score points. Try to avoid the ghosts. If you collect eat a flashing pellet you can then eat the ghosts to score more points. If you run out of lives you can restart the game. You can pause the game by pressing P but the countdown timer will continue. Good luck!
